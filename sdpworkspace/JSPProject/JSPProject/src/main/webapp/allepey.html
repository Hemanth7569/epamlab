Alleppey Tourism

Officially called Alappuzha, Alleppey is known for its network of canals and lagoons with beautiful backwaters and the houseboats offering overnight stays. Located in the southern part of Kerala, the coastline of Alleppey offers some of the best beaches with water sports during the dry season. Alleppey's palm-fringed inter-connect network of canal backwaters attracts a lot of tourists from all over the world.
